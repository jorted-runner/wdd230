Membership Level

Non-Profit

Cost: $100/Year

Benefits: Access to networking events, training sessions, and advertising opportunities tailored for non-profit organizations. Enjoy discounted rates on Chamber events and exclusive access to member resources.

Bronze

Cost: $250/Year

Benefits: Expanded benefits including priority event registration, featured advertising placements on the Chamber's website, and increased visibility within the business community. Bronze members receive exclusive invitations to industry-specific workshops and seminars.

Silver

Cost: $500/Year

Benefits: Silver members receive all Bronze benefits plus enhanced exposure through prominent advertising spots on the Chamber's homepage. Enjoy additional discounts on event sponsorships, personalized marketing support, and exclusive access to VIP networking opportunities.

Gold

Cost: $1,000/Year

Benefits: Gold members receive the highest level of recognition and benefits. Enjoy exclusive sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events, personalized business consulting services, and priority access to leadership development programs. Gold members receive premier advertising placements and enjoy the highest level of visibility within the Chamber community.
